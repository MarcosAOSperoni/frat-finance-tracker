Privacy Policy for Frat Finance Tracker

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

Introduction

Frat Finance Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect the following personal information that you provide when using our app:

  • Contact Information: Email address, full name, and phone number
  • Account Credentials: Email and encrypted password for authentication
  • Financial Information: Dues amounts, payment records, payment dates, and payment methods
  • User-Generated Content: Notes and comments related to payments

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our app, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, and crash reports (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Push Notification Tokens: Device tokens to send payment reminders and notifications (via Firebase Messaging)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features including user authentication, dues tracking, and payment management
  • Notifications: To send payment reminders and important updates about your dues
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our app and improve performance
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access and maintain data security

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Supabase (Backend Database)

  • Purpose: User authentication and data storage
  • Data Stored: All user account information, payment records, and dues data
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: Email/password authentication
  • Firebase Messaging: Push notifications for payment reminders
  • Firebase Analytics: App usage analytics and crash reporting
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Data Storage and Security

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Authentication: Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text
  • Access Control: Data access is restricted based on user roles (Brother or VP of Finance)
  • Server Location: Data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase

While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Within Your Organization: Other members of your fraternity chapter can view financial information according to their role permissions
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Service Providers: With third-party service providers (Supabase, Firebase) necessary to operate our app

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications in your device settings or app preferences

To exercise these rights, contact your chapter's VP of Finance or email us at the address below.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Necessary to provide app services
  • Required for legal or accounting purposes

When you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending a notification through the app
  • Posting the new policy in the App Store listing

Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

User Role-Based Access

Brothers

  • Can view only their own dues and payment history
  • Cannot access other members' financial information

VP of Finance

  • Can view all chapter members' dues and payments
  • Can create dues periods and record payments
  • Has administrative access to chapter financial data
