Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I sign up for the app?
A: Contact your chapter's VP of Finance to receive an invitation code. The app uses invitation-based signup to ensure only chapter members have access.
Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
A: On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and follow the instructions. A password reset link will be sent to your email address.
Q: How do I make a payment?
A: Payments are recorded by your VP of Finance after you pay them via Venmo, Cash, or your chapter's preferred method. Your dues will automatically update once the payment is recorded.
Q: Can I view other members' payment information?
A: No. Brothers can only view their own dues and payment history. Only the VP of Finance has access to all chapter financial information.
Q: How do I enable/disable payment notifications?
A: Go to your Profile → Settings → Notification Preferences. You can customize when and how you receive payment reminders.
Q: I'm the VP of Finance. How do I create a dues period?
A: From the VP Dashboard, tap the "+" button or "Create Dues Period". Enter the semester, total amount, and due date. All active brothers will automatically be assigned these dues.
Q: How do I record a payment for a brother?
A: As VP of Finance, tap on a brother's card, select their dues, and tap "Record Payment". Enter the amount, date, payment method, and any notes.
Q: How do I invite new members?
A: From the VP Dashboard, tap "Invite Member". Enter the new member's email address, and an invitation code will be generated and sent to them.

Technical Issues

Q: The app won't let me log in
A: Try these steps:
  • Verify your email and password are correct
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try resetting your password
  • If problems persist, contact support
Q: I'm not receiving notifications
A: Check these settings:
  • Go to your phone Settings → Notifications → Frat Finance Tracker and enable notifications
  • In the app, check Profile → Notification Preferences
  • Ensure you have an internet connection
Q: My data isn't syncing
A: Make sure you have an active internet connection. Pull down on any screen to refresh. If the issue persists, try logging out and back in.

Privacy & Security
